Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the Advanced Text Modifier Google Docs Add-on ("the Add-on"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Add-on.

Information We Do Not Collect

We believe in privacy by design. The Add-on is built to be completely self-contained within your Google account. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal information or document content. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal data such as your name, email address, or profile information.
  • We do not collect, read, or store the content of your Google Docs documents.
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

How the Add-on Works

All text processing performed by the Add-on happens locally within your browser and your active Google Doc session. The script runs directly on your computer, under the authority of your Google account. At no point is your document data sent to any external servers or third-party services controlled by us.

Permissions

When you install the Add-on, Google will ask for your permission to allow the script to run. This permission is standard for Google Docs Add-ons and is required for the tool to function. It allows the script to:

  • View and manage documents that this application has been installed in: This is necessary for the Add-on to read the text you've selected and replace it with the modified version.

We want to be clear: this permission allows the script to function on your behalf, but it does not give us, the developers, any access to your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through our official homepage.
